list of commands for 'chess bot':

	!board (displays the current board state)
	!RESET (restarts the current board state)
	!count (displays the turn number)
	!move? (displays whos turn to move it is)
	!pieces (displays the positions of every piece in LetterNumber form)
	
How to move:

	!!A1 -> A2
	
		This command moves the piece that is on A1 to B2.
		If the move is illegal then no piece is moved and the players 		turn is not switched
